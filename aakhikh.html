Who We Are

The House of

Our Vision

Created
for Glory

Aakhikh: The House of Blessings is a community of Jesus-lovers located in Guwahati, Assam. Our vision is to see the Name of Jesus fill the earth and to build a Home for all prodigals to return back to the Love of the Father.

We believe the Church is not a building — it is a people. A people marked by love, transformed by grace, and sent into the world as living witnesses of the risen Christ.

Est. 2024 · Guwahati, Assam

What We Believe

Our Core
Values

Christ-Centred

Everything we do flows from and returns to Jesus — the author and finisher of our faith.

Love in Action

Love is not an idea here. It is our practice — toward God, one another, and the lost.

Spirit-Led

We yield to the Holy Spirit in worship, in ministry, and in every decision we make together.

Kingdom Mission

We are not just building a local church — we are building the Kingdom of God on earth.

Leadership

Meet Our
Pastors

Senior Pastor

Rev. Sonia Bhattacharya

Rev. Sonia carries a heart for the lost and the broken. Her preaching is marked by compassion, prophetic clarity, and a deep love for the Word of God. She has been ordained by the grace of God to lead Aakhikh as a place of genuine encounter and healing for all who walk through its doors.

Worship Pastor

Pastor Nabarun Dhar

Pastor Nabarun is a worshipper at heart. His gifting in music and ministry creates an atmosphere where heaven touches earth. As a leader and a servant, he is committed to seeing every person step into the fullness of who God has called them to be through authentic, Spirit-filled worship.
